Font
-Can set the font size at any size
-1px (1/96th inch)
-1pt (1/72nd inch)
-1em (100% of parent)
-1rem (100% root)

Font Weight
-normal bold (keywords)
-lighter bolder (relative to parent)
-number (100-900)
